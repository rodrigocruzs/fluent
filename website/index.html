How it works

A coach in the room. Without anyone in the room.

01

It listens quietly — it never joins the call

No awkward bot sliding into the participant list. Fluent runs on your machine, captures your audio when you allow it, and stays completely invisible to everyone else on the call.

02

It studies how you actually communicate

Hedging, filler, clarity, directness, the moments you take charge and the moments you shrink — analysed in context, across real conversations, not staged exercises.

03

It gives you specific, kind feedback

Not a grade. The one habit worth changing this week, the exact phrases that worked, and a clearer way to say the thing you struggled to say.

04

It builds your communication profile

A living picture of how you come across — strengthening week over week, so you can watch yourself become the communicator you've always known you are.
